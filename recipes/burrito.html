Burrito

Description

Burritos are a popular dish in Mexican cuisine but have gained immense popularity globally. They consist of a large flour tortilla wrapped around a filling, typically including a protein such as beef, chicken, pork, or beans. The fillings can also include rice, beans, cheese, salsa, guacamole, sour cream, lettuce, onions, peppers, and various other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Meat
  • Fillings

Steps

  1. Prepare the filling
  2. Warm tortillas
  3. Assemble the burritos
  4. Heat (optional)
  5. Enjoy
