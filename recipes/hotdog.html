Hot dog

Description

Hot dogs are a popular street food and casual meal enjoyed around the world. They consist of a cooked sausage, typically made from beef, pork, chicken, or a blend of meats, served inside a soft, sliced bun. The sausage is usually pre-cooked and often grilled or boiled. Hot dog buns are soft and elongated, perfectly shaped to cradle the sausage.
