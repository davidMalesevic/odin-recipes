Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish originating from Italy, consisting of a round, flattened wheat-based dough topped with ingredients such as tomato sauce, cheese (often mozzarella), and various toppings like meats, vegetables, and herbs. It's usually baked in an oven, resulting in a delicious, savory meal that's enjoyed worldwide. The versatility of pizza allows for a wide range of flavors and styles, making it a popular choice across different cultures.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees celsius
  2. Stretch out the dough
  3. Add the sauce
  4. Add the mozzarella
  5. Bake for 3-5 minutes or until the crust is crispy and golden-brown
  6. Enjoy
